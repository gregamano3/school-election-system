Alex Rivera

Candidate Manifesto

My Vision for Central High

Hello everyone, I'm Alex. I believe our school council should do more than just plan dances. My three main goals are transparency, mental health, and equity.

Transparency: I will publish the minutes of every council meeting and create a digital suggestion box where you can see the status of your requests in real-time.

Mental Health: I will advocate for 'Rest Weeks' where homework loads are reduced after finals, and work with the administration to bring in more peer counseling resources.

Equity: I want to ensure that smaller clubs like the Art Guild and Coding Club get a fairer share of the student activities fund currently dominated by athletics.

A vote for me is a vote for a council that actually listens. Let's make this year our best one yet.
